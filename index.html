PanCoat

Iconic color to your life

Pancoat Paints India Limited is a dynamic and fast-growing public limited company based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Established in July 2020, the company leverages years of collective industry experience to deliver high-quality, innovative paint solutions across India. With a modern manufacturing facility, Pancoat offers a comprehensive range of products including interior and exterior emulsions, primers, putty, textures, enamels, and waterproofing solutions. Each product reflects the company’s unwavering commitment to quality, durability, and performance. Driven by a passion for excellence and innovation, Pancoat Paints continuously raises the bar with new product launches and cutting-edge technology—earning the trust of customers, professionals, and partners alike. All Products

Pancoat products have a number of important advantages:

High Quality

Pancoat uses only proven raw materials and modern production technologies, which guarantees excellent performance characteristics of their paints and varnishes.

Wide range

The Pancoat line includes materials for various areas of application - from household to industrial. This allows you to select the optimal solution for any task.

Durability

Pancoat coatings are characterized by increased wear resistance and resistance to external influences. They retain their properties for a long time.

Environmental friendliness

All Pancoat products comply with modern safety standards and do not contain harmful substances.

Ease of use

Pancoat paints and varnishes are easy to use and do not require special skills.

Due to these advantages, Pancoat products are in high demand among professionals and private consumers. They provide reliable protection and an attractive appearance on any surfaces.
